how it works
• set-up in front of your house
• decide who benefits~you, school, team, charity, etc.
• participating addresses will be ‘pinned’ on a GoogleMap and made available via ???

Signs are saved and reused...please, do not discard!

• post on Facebook, Instagram, Tweet, Snapchat, etc.
